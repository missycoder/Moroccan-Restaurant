Who We Are

Marhaba! Tajine Moroccan Restaurant brings quality, authentic Moroccan cuisines in Singapore! From the vibrant spices to the delicate sweetness of Moroccan pastries, each bite is a journey through the enchanting landscapes and culinary traditions of Morocco.

Our Mission

At Tajine Moroccan Restaurant, our mission is to transport you to the heart of Morocco with every dish we serve. We are committed to provide an exceptional culinary experience, where the flavours tell a story, and each meal is a celebration of tradition, and community.

Our Vision

To be Singapore's #1 for those seeking a quality, and authentic Moroccan dining experience. We aim to be recognized not only for the excellence of our cuisine but also for the cultural richness we bring to the local culinary scene.

Our Values

Rooted with quality, authenticity, and great hospitality - Tajine Moroccan Restaurant sources the finest ingredients direct from Morocco. Our values ensure that every dish reflects the true flavours of Morocco, creating an atmosphere where guests feel genuinely embraced by the warmth of Moroccan traditions.
